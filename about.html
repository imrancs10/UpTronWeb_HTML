HISTORY OF THE COMPANY

UPTRON FORBES LIMITED was incorporated under the companies’ act 1956, on the day of 30th April 1977 later under section 21 of companies’ act 1956 and approval of central government changed their name to “UPTRON POWERTRONICS LIMITED” on 18th September 1978. UPTRON POWERTRONICS LIMITED in 1977 works under the administrative control of department of IT & Electronics Government of Uttar Pradesh, Once of the leading provider in the state of art product in the field of IT & Electronics. Specially in IT Hardware, Software, Survieliance System, Manpower, Outsourching & Training to government institutes.
