HISTORY OF COMPANY

UPTRON FORBES LIMITED was incorporated under the companies’ act 1956, on the day of 30th April 1977 later under section 21 of companies’ act 1956 and approval of central government changed their name to “UPTRON POWERTRONICS LIMITED” on 18th September 1978.

Objective for which the company is established are:

II. The objects for which the Company is established are:

A) Main Objects of the Company to be pursued by the Company on its incorporation :

(1) To carry on all or any of the business of manufacturers, installers, maintainers, repairers of and dealers in electrical and electronic apparatus and instruments of every description including battery chargers. inverters, rectifiers, regulated power suppliers. automatic voltage regulators and other applied equipments of and in electronic components, radio, television and tele-communication requisites and supplies, including relays. transformers, electric accumulators, chokes, switches, lamps. printed circuits and generally wireless and electrical components, fittings, parts, apparatus, accessories and equipments of every kind.

(2) To manufacture, buy, import, export, put up, sell, use, install, hire, let on hire, repair, assemble, distribute or otherwise deal in or trade in telephone components, telegraphs (wireless or other) dynamos. engines, accumulators, transformers, condensers, capacitors, cables, resistances, amplifiers, transmitters, chokes, cables, wires, lamps, dry cells, batteries, torches lighters and all appliances, apparatus, machinery, goods, gadgets, accessories, articles or things which may hereafter be invented or connected with the generation, accumulation, distribution, supply or employment of electricity or any power, force or energy that can be used as substitute therefore including all cables, wires, or appliances for connecting apparatus at a distance with the other apparatus and including the formation, maintenance and alterations of exchange and Centres.

2A) To undertake and carry on the business of manufacturer, installers, maintainers, repairers and dealers of fire fighting equipments security and surveillance related products and other related items.

2B) To undertake business activities related to Software, development center, IT data center, Cloud services and others CAD / CAM training centre, manufacturing of electronics equipments / IT / ITES training.

3) To carry on the business of electricians, electrical engineers, electrical contractors, and manufacturers of all kinds of electrical machinery telephonic or telegraphic apparatus of any kind.

4) To carry on business works and activities related to software development , Electronic Data Processing Systems, Data Communication systems, Hardware Software, Networking, Internet, e-commerce. et-cetera

5) To carry on business, Works and activities in the field of Information Technology and its enables services e.g.

a) Value added IT Services:

1. Web enability Legacy systems

2. E-Commerce/Extended Enterprises Application

3. Standards-based Application integration

4. Knowledge Management

5. Conveyance Application

b) Software Products

1. Emerging Silver

2. Productization

3. Embedded Software

c) It Enabled Services

1. HR Services

2. Remote Customer Interaction

3. Data Search, Integration and Analysis

4. Engineering and Design Services

d) E-Business

1. Domestic to Domestic Business

2. NRI oriented Business to Consumer

6. To undertake activities for Computer Education/Corporate Training and proliferation knowledge, know-how, know-why and information about the Information Technology.

The authorized share capital of the company is Rs 4,50,00,000 ( four crores fifty lakhs only) divided into 4,50,000 equity shares of Rs 100 each.
